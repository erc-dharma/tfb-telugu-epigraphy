Commentary

Bibliography

The inscription was noted in A. R. No. 337 of 1922 and first published by J. Ramayya Pantulu (1948: page 336, № 616). K. M. Sastri re-edits that edition and provides a summary: Refers to a gift of pannasa to brahmins of the Bhāradwāja gōtra and contains the imprecation that he who acts unjustly against it will incur the sin of having killed a thousand brahmins at Baranasi1969, page 335, № 73.
