Grant of a tank of Apimanagaṁgukessaraju at Būḍidigaḍḍapalli (Vaidumba?)

Metadata

Identifier:

Telugu_Vaidumba_SIIX633

Edition

1svasti śrī vaṇavṟōla apimanagaṁgu kessaraju-
2n iccina datti i ceṟuvuḷa vitara(ṭṭi) ve-
3ṭanivāru baranasiyu ceṟuvu kavila-
4yun aḻisinavāri (pā)paṁ(bu) goṇḍṟu veṭaniva-
5ru bu(ṭe)limarammacari sēse

Translation by Sastri (1969)

Hail! Prosperity! Apimanagaṁgu Kessaraju made the grant at Vaṇavrōlu. Those who do not (protect?) these tanks will incur the sin of those who destroy Baranasi, a tank and a cow. Buṭelimarammacari did (i.e. engraved this inscription).

Translation by Jens Thomas

Commentary

The text follows the edition of J. Ramayya Pantulu in 1948: 341, № 633 in absence of a picture.

Bibliography

The inscription was noted in A. R. No. 811 of 1917 and first published by J. Ramayya Pantulu in 1948: 341, № 633 with few metadata and without translation.  K. M. Sastry 1969: 341 provides a translation while relying on J. Ramayya Pantulu’s edition.
