Bibliography

The inscription was noted in A. R. No. 278 of 1922 and first published by J. Ramayya Pantulu without a picture (1948: 19, № 48). K. M. Sastri provided a summary: "Records the name Nāranarāndra" (1969: 328, № 59) .
