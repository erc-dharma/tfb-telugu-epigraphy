1svasti śrī paṭṭapināṁṭṭi visi-
2kuṟiki paṟuṇḍu ma(ṭa)mva vē(yu)-
3ratō koni pō(yeṅka śrī jē)namaha-
4ntadhavalunṛpatu dhanirāsim i-
5ccinaṇḍu

Translation by Jens Thomas

Commentary

The text follows the edition of H. Krishna Sastri (1925: 414, № 1128) in absence of a picture.

Bibliography

The text was edited together with a second inscription on the same stone by H. Krishna Sastri in 1925: 414, № 1128 without a picture. The text was re-published by K. M. Sastri (1969: 345, № 98) on basis of SII with one error and without translation.
