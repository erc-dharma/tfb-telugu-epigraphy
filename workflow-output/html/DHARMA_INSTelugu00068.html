Commentary

Bibliography

The inscription was noted in A. R. No. 515 of 1906 and first published by J. Ramayya Pantulu (1948: 335, № 614). K. M. Sastri re-edited the text of that edition and provided a summary: "States that a certain Abhiccuṟamganṟu fought a battle at Muttukūru with wrestlers (malluru)" (Sastri 1969: 335) .
