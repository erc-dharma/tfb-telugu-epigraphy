Remarks: Gu(ja)pōṟēru is mentioned who may have been a ruler. The date is given according to Radha Krishna who assigns it on palaeographical grounds after having seen the estampage (1971: 47, n. 83). If Gu(ja)pōṟēru is identical with Kūcapōṟiyāru of EI XXVII, 42 K the date would be around 700 CE if the conjectural reading of the ruler ibidem as Vikramāditya is correct. The inscription was published without a picture.
