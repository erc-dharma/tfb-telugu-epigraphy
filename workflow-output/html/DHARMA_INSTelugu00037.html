Name of Śrī Śailamuni

Metadata

Identifier:

INSTelugu00037

Edition

1śrī śailamuni

Translation by Jens Thomas

Śrī Śailamuni

Commentary

The name was found on a beam in the verandah of the seventh cave temple at BhairavakoṇḍaRamayya Pantulu 1948, page 19. The nature of the name (≈ “holy man of the mountain (perh. Himalaya)" may rather point towards a high ranking religious person who visited the place than towards a craftsman who worked there; he might, however, have been a donor as well.

Bibliography

The inscription was noted in A. R. No. 278 of 1922 and was first edited by J. Ramayya Pantulu with few metadata and without picture (1948: page 19, № 53).

Secondary
