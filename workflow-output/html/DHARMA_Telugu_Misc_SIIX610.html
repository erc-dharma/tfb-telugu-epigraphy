Bibliography

The inscription was noted in A. R. No. 469 of 1906 and first published by J. Ramayya Pantulu (1948: 334, № 610). K. M. Sastri re-editet the text based on that edition and provided a summary: "Refers to the son of a certain Puliyaḍiyamakanṟu, ruler of Emmuduru."
