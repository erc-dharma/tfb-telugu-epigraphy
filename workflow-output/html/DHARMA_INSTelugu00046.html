The name of Śrī Rāmunṟu

Metadata

Identifier:

INSTelugu00046

Edition

1śrī rāmunṟu

Translation by Jens Thomas

Commentary

The inscription was found on a pillar that also contains other personal names. Hence, a person’s name (rather than that of the god) was inscribed here.
